DATA GAPS, LIMITATIONS & METHOD NOTES:
- Missing Data: Many concrete player metrics (exact sales figures, detailed demographics) are not publicly
available. We relied on proxy indicators: Steam review counts, chart positions, and anecdotal evidence. For
example, SteamSpy ranges gave ballpark owner counts but require cautious interpretation (SteamSpy
is not perfectly accurate post-2018 privacy changes). No official breakdown of sales by region or platform
was accessible; where we mentioned such, it’s inferred (⚠ estimated from context or partial data like
language of reviews or platform popularity). Also, engagement metrics on newer platforms (TikTok views,

Discord membership) are partially hidden or ephemeral – e.g., Discord doesn’t show member counts openly
beyond a certain limit (the 333 figure for r/TheChineseRoom was found via a third-party and might be
slightly outdated ). We flagged uncertain figures with ⚠ or to denote estimation or sparse evidence.
